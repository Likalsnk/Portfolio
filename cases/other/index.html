Concept

RefillArena aims to bridge the gap between traditional gaming top-ups and modern Web3 assets. The dark UI reduces eye strain for gamers, while the vibrant accent colors highlight call-to-actions.

UX Focus

Speed is key. The checkout flow was optimized to require only 3 clicks from browsing to payment completion. Micro-interactions (like the glitch effect) add a layer of immersion.
