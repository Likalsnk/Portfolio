The Challenge

Translating the intangible concept of "love languages" into a tangible, digital experience. The goal was to create a warm, inviting interface that feels personal and safe without being overly cloying.

Visual Direction

I chose a soft palette of pinks and warm whites, paired with rounded typography to evoke gentleness. The card-based interaction mimics the feeling of playing a game or drawing a tarot card.
